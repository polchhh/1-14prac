INSTRUCTIONS FOR CHOOSING A RING

Use a Ring Size Chart: There's a standard ring size chart based on diameters in millimeters. You can usually find this online or from a jeweler.

Measure the Finger: The best way to determine size is by measuring the inner diameter of your ring or the diameter of the finger where you'll wear it. This helps accurately determine the required size.

Consider Circumstances: If it's a gift and you can't measure the recipient's finger, inquire about the size of another ring they wear or use tools like invisible sizers.

Consult a Jeweler: If you have doubts or questions, it's best to seek professional advice. Jewelers can offer guidance and assistance in choosing the right size.
